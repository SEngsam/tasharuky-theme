Renovating classrooms and equipping learning spaces

Project Details

  • Education Campaign

This project supports the renovation of classrooms and the purchase of essential learning equipment for 250 students. The campaign focuses on improving the daily learning environment with safer rooms, functional desks, boards, and basic digital tools that help teachers deliver lessons more effectively.

Tasharuky presents the project details, funding progress, and implementation updates so supporters can follow how their donations move from contribution to measurable impact.

Goal $25,000
Raised $17,250
Remaining $7,750
Funding Progress
69%

Expected Impact

The project is designed to improve the quality, safety, and continuity of education for students who need stable learning spaces.

Better Learning Spaces

Classrooms will be repaired, cleaned, painted, and prepared for daily use.

Essential Equipment

Students and teachers receive desks, boards, supplies, and learning tools.

Implementation Plan

The project follows a clear process to make sure donations are translated into visible, trackable results.

The implementing partner reviews the current classroom condition, confirms priority repairs, and prepares a procurement list.

  • Site assessment, beneficiary verification, and budget confirmation.

Repairs, painting, furniture installation, and equipment delivery are completed according to the approved project plan.

  • Renovation work, supplier coordination, and delivery tracking.

Supporters receive updates showing what was completed, how funds were used, and how many students benefited.

  • Progress photos, completion notes, and final impact reporting.
